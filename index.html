About SproutIT

Leadership is about being poised, motivated and a lot more. You need a team with an astute mindset to win through the challenges and reach your goals. We all have a spark to be the best. At Sprout’IT you are pushed to your limits to vie and and be the crème de la crème.

About iTELF

iTELF is the Entrepreneurship Cell of SCIT which provides a dais for upcoming IT Entrepreneurs and Leaders to explore the world of start-ups, exchange ideas, foster and enhance their leadership skills. This forum helps the entrepreneurs of today to metamorphose into victorious leaders of tomorrow.

The Innovation Oasis

“The Innovation Oasis” embraces the essence of creativity and perseverance as we explore how these Business titans have disrupted industries and shaped the future. The journey of businesses that started as humble sparks but ignited into roaring flames, leaving a lasting impact on their markets and communities. Despite limited resources, they demonstrate how passion, strategic thinking, and the ability to adapt can drive significant change. Each Step towards creation is a step towards success.

Meet The Speakers

AJIT HATTI

PureID, Pune

Ajit Hatti is a serial inventor and innovator and founder of PureID. He has been developing enterprise class security products over a decade and a half. Ajit regularly presents his research & tools at conferences like Black Hat and DEF CON. He is also the co-founder of Null - Open Security Community & is the founder of Blockchain Village which he organizes at DEF CON.

JIGNESH PATEL

VaccineOnWheels, Pune

Jignesh Patel is a serial entrepreneur with 15+ years of leadership experience. In his second inning as a social enterpreneur, he started journey with first service innovation - India's first Doctor-based Mobile Vaccination Clinic - VaccineOnWheel; and has proudly served more than 2.5 Million hard-to-reach people within 40+ Districts in 6 States in India in partnership with Government, International Foundations and Corporates.

PAYAL JAIN

Funngro, Mumbai

Shark Tank India season 2 fame, Payal Jain is the founder and CEO of Funngro. An IIM Calcutta Alumni, having work experience of more than 20 years in industry in companies like Wipro, Capgemini, Syntel, Worldline Global. Her journey from just an idea to a successful venture, already helping 8,00,000 plus Teenagers with help of 2500 companies. Her experience of building a company and creating large impact on our country's future is really inspiring.



CHIRAG PANCHAL

Enerlyf, Ahmedabad

Chirag Panchal, B.Tech Mechatronics graduate and visionary founder, is a recipient of the Leaders in Innovation Fellowship from the Royal Academy of Engineering, London, sponsored by the UK Government. Chirag leads ENERLYF, a climate-conscious tech company curbing cooling wastage and enhancing thermal comfort, achieving a remarkable 20-35% energy efficiency boost. He's not only an innovator but also a fitness enthusiast who heads the 5AM club.

VIKRANT VARTAK

Pune

Author of two books: Guideposts and 50 Dilemmas; Vikrant Vartak is the Managing Director of The Senate Business Center and the Founder of Mavens. Spread over 21,000 sft through its two branches in Erandwane & Wakad, Vikrant's mainstay The Senate Business Center is one of the prominent workplace solution providers in Pune. His brainchild Mavens is a pioneer CEO Peer group. Mavens' is engaged in fostering entrepreneurship through various initiatives like Talk Shows, Mavens Huddle, Mavens Good Read etc.

Events

Ad-Mad

AD MAD is an 'Advertising Campaign Competition' for Business students to showcase their creative business skills in marketing products and services. Students will act out on various amusing, attention-grabbing, and conceptual advertisement scripts and ideas relevant to different sectors, including FMCG products, luxury goods and services, automobiles, social awareness, etc.

Rural Le Carte

It's time to choose, combine, construct, and plan from various rural products. It's time NOW! to showcase your skills if you want rural India to develop like urban India and want to be a part of that transformation.



The Entrepreneurship Film Festival

Immerse yourself in curated documentaries and movies showcasing the journeys of remarkable entrepreneurs and startups. Join us for valuable discussions, networking, and inspiration. Unleash your entrepreneurial potential.

The Brand Challenge: Revitalizing Failing Enterprises

Tackle actual failed business problems, develop thorough turnaround plans, and demonstrate your strategic prowess. Participate in this immersive event of creativity and problem-solving to compete, invent, and redefine success.



Strategic Spectrum

This competition is a fusion of creative marketing and perspective-based strategy. Each team will be assigned a unique colored "thinking cap," symbolizing a specific perspective. With designated parameters for each cap, teams will craft imaginative marketing campaigns for fictional companies.

Money Fusion

aking inspiration from the classic Stanford University classroom, This exercise ensures that the young generation has the skills to capitalize on today's opportunities.
